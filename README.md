# मार्गदर्शिनी — MargDarshini

> 🥇 **1st Place — Internal Smart India Hackathon (SIH)**

**MargDarshini** (Sanskrit: *"Guide"*) is an AI-powered career guidance platform designed to make students job-ready. It bridges the gap between education and employment by offering personalised career direction, resume tools, interview prep, and skill development — all in one place.

---

## The Problem We Solved

Millions of students graduate each year without a clear sense of direction — unsure which career suits them, how to present themselves, or what skills the job market actually demands. MargDarshini tackles this head-on with a suite of AI-assisted tools that guide students from confusion to career clarity.

---

## Platform Services

| Service | What It Does |
|---|---|
| 🎯 Career Quiz | Suggests personalised career paths based on education, skills, and interests |
| 📄 Resume Analyzer | Reviews your resume and highlights areas to improve |
| 🛠️ Resume Builder | Helps you build a professional resume from scratch |
| 🗺️ Pathway Planning | Maps out a step-by-step roadmap to your target career |
| 📊 Job Market Analysis | Shows which sectors are hiring and what skills they demand |
| 🎤 Interview Preparation | Prepares students for real-world interviews with practice tools |
| 📚 Skill Development | Recommends resources to upskill based on your career goals |

---

## How the Career Quiz Works

Students answer 4 quick questions:

1. **Education level** — 10th, 12th, Diploma, UG, PG
2. **Skills** — Programming, Communication, Data Analysis, Leadership, and more
3. **Sector interest** — IT, Healthcare, Finance, Education, Government, and more
4. **Work preference** — Urban, Rural, Remote, or Open

The platform then suggests tailored career paths based on their unique profile.
